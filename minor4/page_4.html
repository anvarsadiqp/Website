FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Can I use my existing domain name with a Siteinnovo site?


Yes. You can map an existing domain to your SiteInnovo site, or you can purchase a domain from us.


What is a subdomain?


Your url will include '.siteinnovo.com'.


Can I create an email with a domain I own?


No, you can only set up Siteinnovo email accounts with a domain purchased from Siteinnovo. If you already have your own domain, you can easily use it for your new Siteinnovo site, and create an email account with another provider.


Can I create different email accounts with the same domain?


Yes, you can create as many as you need, and it is very easy to

set this up.


Do Siteinnovo sites have good Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

performance?


Siteinnovo websites are built in HTML and CSS, rather than Flash,

which means they are naturally search engine friendly.
